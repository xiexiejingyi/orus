The Vision

A young, bold and relatable RTD brand — one you grab because it looks fun, and keep because you actually know what's inside.

Most canned cocktails are either sterile "craft" clones or loud energy-drink knock-offs. ORUS RTD sits in the middle: the confidence and honesty of the bar, translated into packaging that feels like it belongs in a young person's fridge, festival cooler, or on a party table. The inspirations below are the tone we're chasing — and how ORUS can do it our own way.

YoungPlayful, unserious energy. Hand-drawn marks, oversized type, saturated colour. It should feel made by people our age, not a legacy distillery.
BoldOne idea per can, at maximum scale. The abstracted ORUS Seal becomes a graphic billboard — instant recognition across a crowded shelf or a dark bar.
RelatableCurious names spark the pick-up; plain-language flavour + ABV close the deal. No pretension, no guessing. Honest value, just like the bar.

Signature Design

Pulling the inspirations together into one recommended direction. The move: take the two ideas that make Strangers work — hazy airbrushed gradient waves and a bold, say-it-out-loud name — and swap their generic candy graphic for our own asset: the ORUS Seal, exploded. Cropped this large, the seal reads as a giant "O" — the start of ORUS and the shape of an open mouth, an open door, a portal. It's unmistakably ours without ever printing the full logo.

  • The GraphicExploded Seal. One crop of the seal, blown up until it bleeds off every edge. Doubles as the "O" of ORUS. This is the hero — no small logo needed.
  • The BackdropHazy gradient waves (borrowed from Strangers) in a single Status Colour fading to bone. Warm, retro, alive — softens the hard geometry of the seal.
  • The NameA mood, said out loud. Set big in the lower third in a slightly retro condensed sans. "Hot & Bothered", "Slow Hands" — cheeky, memorable, human.
  • The Clarity LinePlain flavour + ABV directly under the name: "YUZU & UME SHOCHU SPRITZ · 5.5% · 330ML". Utilitarian type. You always know exactly what you're drinking.
  • The WordmarkORUS, small and confident at the top. The seal carries recognition; the wordmark just signs it.
  • FinishMatte body, spot-gloss seal. Colour does the shouting; the tactile contrast makes it feel premium in the hand.
The system, unrolled — one template, four Status Colours. Because the seal and layout stay fixed while only the colour + name + flavour change, the range reads as one bold family on the shelf and scales to endless seasonal drops.

Seal Toolkit

The exploded "O" is the hero, but the seal is a flexible asset — it can be cropped, cut, tiled, pressed or split. Here are more ways to build a can around it, so the range never feels one-note and can flex across flavours, limited editions and premium tiers.

01 · The Window
The seal's oval hole becomes a peephole onto macro photography of the actual ingredients — limes, mint, splashing soda. Instantly appetising and tells you the flavour without reading a word. Great for the fruit-forward SKUs.
02 · The Monogram
The seal tiled into an all-over tonal pattern — logomania, but subtle. Adds tactile texture and a premium, ownable "house print" that works on cans, coasters, tees and totes alike.
03 · The Emboss
On bare brushed aluminum, the seal is physically pressed into the metal — no ink, tone-on-tone, catching the light. The quiet, premium end of the range for a limited or top-tier release.

More levers to explore — the seal as a die-cut can-tab / lid stamp; stretched tall to elongate the can; used as a progress meter where the oval fills up with colour by ABV or flavour intensity; or turned into a scratch-panel / QR frame on the back linking to a playlist or the bar. One shape, endless system.

Positioning & Scenarios

产品定位与消费场景 (Product Positioning & Scenarios)
The RTD line extends ORUS's "Third Space" philosophy beyond the physical bar. It's designed to be a catalyst for connection in any setting, translating the bar's energy into a portable format.

Single Drinking (单人饮用)
For winding down at home or creative work sessions. Low-ABV or tea-based options (like Left On Read) that offer complex flavors without the heavy hit.
Party & Social (聚会场景)
High-energy, conversation-starting cans to bring to a dinner party or house gathering. The bold names act as literal icebreakers.
Retail Display (零售陈列)
Designed for shelf impact. When lined up in a fridge or retail shelf, the massive geometric shapes and vibrant ORUS brand colors create a striking, billboard-like blocking effect.

Colours & The Seal

Status Colours 与 Seal 的应用 (Application of Status Colours & Seal)
We do not just stamp the ORUS logo on the can. Instead, we use the brand's core visual assets as architectural building blocks for the packaging art.

  • Exploding the MotifThe seal, zoomed in 1000%. We keep only the outer curve and the oval negative space, so it reads as a giant "O" — instantly ownable, no full logo needed.
  • Molten · On FireThe high-energy spritz. Loud, flirty, the life of the room.
  • Verdigris · OpenThe refreshing icebreaker. Cool, approachable, down for anything.
  • Sulphur · WaitingThe easy social sipper. Sweet, sunny, no rush.
  • Ore Black · DNDThe low-ABV wind-down. Deep, grounding, in your own world.
  • Why it worksOne seal, four states, instant clarity. The same logic as the bar's Status Coasters — the colour tells you the mood before you even read the name.
