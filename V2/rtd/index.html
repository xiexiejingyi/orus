01

Positioning & Scenarios

产品定位与消费场景 (Product Positioning & Scenarios)
The RTD line extends ORUS's "Third Space" philosophy beyond the physical bar. It's designed to be a catalyst for connection in any setting, translating the bar's energy into a portable format.

Single Drinking (单人饮用)
For winding down at home or creative work sessions. Low-ABV or tea-based options (like Quiet Escape) that offer complex flavors without the heavy hit.
Party & Social (聚会场景)
High-energy, conversation-starting cans to bring to a dinner party or house gathering. The bold names act as literal icebreakers.
Retail Display (零售陈列)
Designed for shelf impact. When lined up in a fridge or retail shelf, the massive geometric shapes and vibrant ORUS brand colors create a striking, billboard-like blocking effect.
02

Design & Format

包装设计方向与形式 (Packaging Direction & Format)
Ready-to-drink is a crowded space. To stand out, we bypass traditional "craft cocktail" tropes (intricate illustrations, vintage fonts) and lean into an ultra-modern, editorial, and highly graphic identity.

Can vs. Bottle (罐装／瓶装形式)
Primary Format: Sleek Aluminum Cans (330ml).
Cans are lighter, chill faster, are vastly superior for outdoor/party scenarios, and offer a continuous 360-degree canvas for our bold graphics. They feel fresh, young, and unpretentious.

(Glass bottles may be reserved later for premium, large-format pre-batched spirits like the ORUS Negroni, but the core RTD lifestyle line lives in cans).
Curiosity + Clarity
The Naming: Lead with intrigue. Names like "Strangers' Hard Candy" spark curiosity.

The Specs: Ground it instantly. Underneath the bold name, the exact flavor profile sits in clean, utilitarian type (e.g., "YUZU & UME SHOCHU - 5.5% VOL"). No guessing what you're buying.
03

Colors & The Seal

Status Colours 与 Seal 的应用 (Application of Status Colours & Seal)
We do not just stamp the ORUS logo on the can. Instead, we use the brand's core visual assets as architectural building blocks for the packaging art.

Exploding the Motif
Abstracted Seal: The ORUS Seal is zoomed in 1000%. We only see the curve of the top boundary and the oval negative space. It becomes a bold, modern, and instantly ownable geometric pattern that wraps the entire can.

Status Colours mapping: We borrow the Status Coasters logic from the bar. Each flavor's energy matches a color status. Molten (On Fire) for high-energy spritz. Verdigris (Open) for a refreshing icebreaker. Sulphur (Waiting/Wingman) for an easy-going social drink.
