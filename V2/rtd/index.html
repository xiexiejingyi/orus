Strategy & Naming

Ready-to-drink is crowded. To stand out, we rely on the same strategy as the bar: Honest Value and Real Connection. The design is young, bold, and highly graphic, taking the ORUS Seal and exploding its scale so it becomes an abstract geometric pattern wrapping the can. The typography strategy is two-fold: a playful, curious name that sparks conversation, paired immediately with straightforward, clear ingredients.

The Layout: Curiosity + Clarity
The Graphic: The ORUS Seal is zoomed in 1000%. We only see the curve of the top boundary and the oval negative space. It's bold, modern, and instantly ownable without just pasting a small logo on the front.

The Naming: Lead with intrigue. Names like "Strangers' Hard Candy" or "Liquid Icebreaker" make people want to pick it up.

The Specs: Immediately ground it. Underneath the bold name is the exact flavour profile in clean, utilitarian type: "YUZU & UME SHOCHU SPRITZ - 5.5% VOL". No guessing what you're buying.
